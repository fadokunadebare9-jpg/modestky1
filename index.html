The Process

How Modestky works

01

Browse & Pick

See designers near you sorted by distance. Each listing shows real photos and a live delivery window.

02

Pay Into Escrow

You pay — but your money goes into a protected vault, not to the designer. It waits until you're satisfied.

03

Courier Arrives

A vetted courier picks it up and comes to your gate. Live tracking the whole way. Under 60 minutes.

04

Try On, Then Decide

Courier waits. Love it? Tap "It's a fit" and payment releases. Doesn't work? Hand it back, full refund.
